CSDP is a 15-month development programme by CSA that equips recent graduates and mid-career professionals with cybersecurity skills and knowledge. The programme's aim is to effectively build the cybersecurity capabilities in the public sector and keep Singapore's cyberspace safe and secure. In the first three months, CSDP officers will undergo classroom training with CSA Academy, SUTD and Ngee Ann Polytechnic as well as acquiring industry certifications such as EC-Council's CEH & Cisco Cyber Ops Associate.
